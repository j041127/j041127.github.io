Bauhaus Timeline

  • 1919

    발터 그로피우스(Walter Gropius)는 독일 바이마르에 공예와 미술을 결합한 바우하우스를 설립한다.

  • 1923

    The Bauhaus adopts a new focus on modern industrial design and functionality.

  • 1925

    The Bauhaus moves to Dessau, where it achieves international acclaim for its design principles.

  • 1933

    The Nazi regime forces the closure of the Bauhaus, but its influence spreads worldwide.
